The product thinking phase focused on identifying real-world ecommerce problems and designing an AI-first shopping experience capable of improving user interaction and product discovery.

Analyzed challenges in traditional ecommerce systems such as:
- overwhelming product listings
- poor personalization

- filter-heavy navigation
- lack of conversational interaction
